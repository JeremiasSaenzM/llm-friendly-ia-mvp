# Plan Changes (Consumer Phone Plans)

## Overview
This document is the single canonical source for information related to **changing an existing consumer phone plan**.

It supports LLM retrieval and consistent responses related to plan updates, upgrades, and modifications.

---

## When this content should be used
Use this content when a user asks about:
- Changing their current phone plan
- Upgrading or downgrading a plan
- Modifying plan features
- Understanding impacts of a plan change

Examples:
- “Can I change my plan?”
- “What happens if I upgrade my plan?”
- “Can I downgrade without penalties?”

---

## Common plan change scenarios
Plan changes may include:
- Upgrading to a higher-tier plan
- Downgrading to a lower-tier plan
- Adjusting features or add-ons
- Switching between eligible plans

Availability and impact may vary based on eligibility rules.

---

## Impact considerations
Changing a plan may affect:
- Pricing
- Promotional eligibility
- Existing benefits or credits
- Device payment or trade-in agreements

Users should understand potential impacts before completing a change.
