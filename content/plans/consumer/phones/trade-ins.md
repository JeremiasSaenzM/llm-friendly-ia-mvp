# Trade-ins (Consumer Phone Plans)

## Overview
This document is the single canonical source for information related to **device trade-ins** within **consumer phone plans**.

- Large Language Model (LLM) retrieval

## When this content should be used
Use this content when a user asks about:
- Trade-in eligibility

- How a trade-in affects a phone plan

Examples:
- “Can I trade in my phone?”
- “Does a trade-in change my plan?”
- “Am I eligible for a trade-in with my current plan?”

---

## Trade-in eligibility (high level)
Trade-in eligibility typically depends on:
- The condition of the device being traded in
- The type of plan the customer is on
- Whether the device meets minimum requirements set by the offer

Specific eligibility rules may vary by promotion or device type.

---

## Trade-in process (simplified)
At a high level, the trade-in process usually includes:
1. Verifying device eligibility
2. Selecting a trade-in option during plan selection or change
3. Submitting or returning the device following provided instructions
4. Applying any eligible credits or benefits to the plan or account

---

## Impact on the plan
A trade-in may:
- Enable access to promotional pricing
- Apply credits over time
- Require maintaining an eligible plan for a defined period

Trade-in benefits are tied to the plan associated with the transaction.
